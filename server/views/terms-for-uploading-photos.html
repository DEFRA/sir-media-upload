By uploading any photos to the Report an environmental problem service you agree to these terms.

Photos you upload

You will only upload photos that:

  • you own or have the right to upload from the owner
  • do not infringe copyright, privacy, or other legal rights
  • are relevant to a report made using the Report an environmental problem service
  • are accurate to the best of your knowledge and are not deliberately misleading

Photos you must not upload

You will not upload photos that:

  • include personal data (for example identifiable individuals or number plates) not relevant to a report made using the Report an environmental problem service
  • are offensive, abusive or defamatory
  • inappropriately feature children or vulnerable individuals
  • could risk anyone's personal safety

Photos that do not meet these rules may be referred to law enforcement or other legal authorities.

How we use your photos

Photos will be attached to the relevant report made using the Report an environmental problem service. This includes any personal data given when the report was made.

The Environment Agency will:

  • view, copy, store, and analyse the photo
  • store the photo for up to 12 months

Photos will only be used by authorised EA personnel to investigate a potential environmental incident, as part of their official duties.

We will not share your photos with anyone else unless we have to do so by law.

Photos are stored and processed in accordance with our privacy notice.

You can remove consent for us to use an image at any time by contacting:

Email: enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk
Telephone: 03708 506 506
